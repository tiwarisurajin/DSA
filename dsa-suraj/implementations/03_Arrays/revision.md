## How to revise a problem properly

Don't re-read your old solution first. Re-derive it:
1. Re-read only the **Problem Statement** section.
2. Try to solve it fresh, brute force first.
3. Only then compare against your optimized writeup.
4. If you needed a hint, log it in [`mistakes.md`](./mistakes.md) if it's a new mistake, and reset that problem's interval to Day 1.
