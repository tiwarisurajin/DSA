## How this works

1. Every topic folder has its own `revision.md` with a per-problem spaced-repetition tracker (Day 1 → 3 → 7 → 21 → 60).
2. Once a week, scan all topic `revision.md` files for anything due, and copy the due items into "This Week" above.
3. After revising, update both this file AND the topic's own `revision.md`.
